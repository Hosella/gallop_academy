About our school

At Gallop Academy, we believe that riding is not only a sport, but also an art that creates harmony and trust between horse and rider. Our school has been in operation since 2003. Our team works to provide you with the knowledge and skills that will help you become a skilled rider and expand your opportunities in the world of riding. We teach both adults and children.
